About Our Coffee Beans

Coffee beans, the seeds of the Coffea plant, come in two main types—Arabica and Robusta. Arabica offers a mild, nuanced flavor with higher acidity, thriving at cooler elevations. Robusta, hardier and bolder, boasts a stronger taste and more caffeine, perfect for espresso blends. The processing methods, such as washed or natural, contribute to diverse flavor profiles. Roasting transforms beans from light to dark, influencing their complexity. From the high altitudes to the roasting chamber, coffee beans offer a rich and varied sensory journey in every cup.
